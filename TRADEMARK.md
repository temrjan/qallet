## Forks

The AGPL-3.0-or-later license grants you the right to fork and modify the
source code. **This right does not extend to the Marks.** A public fork
must be renamed and rebranded before distribution.

Acceptable examples:

- "MyWallet (based on Rustok)"
- "Acme Wallet — powered by a txguard-derived engine"

Unacceptable examples:

- "Rustok Plus", "Rustok Pro", "Rustok Fork"
- An app named "Rustok" published by anyone other than the copyright
  holder
- A product using the Rustok logo
